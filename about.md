## Rafael G. Madrigal

I expect to complete my M.Sc. in Data Science degree (x3 Dean’s Lister) at the Asian Institute of Management (AIM) in June 2021. AIM is an international research and management institution co-founded by Harvard Business School. After graduation, I intend to guide firms in crafting their respective digital strategies as they venture into the 4th Industrial Revolution and how they can harness the power of data for Social Good.

Back at work, I combined my technical and business expertise to guide middle and senior leadership through the rigorous cycle of corporate planning (strategy, business planning) and KPI management. In 2019, my outstanding work earned me the President’s Pride due to Performance (P3) award (equivalent to "employee of the year"), the highest recognition possible for my level at my firm.

I am also engaged with the AIM-TeaM Energy Center for Bridging Leadership, Freidrich-Ebert-Stiftung Philippines, 2030 Youth Force in the Philippines, Reboot Philippines, and International Water Association.

My data projects are mostly centered on Natural Language Processing, Advanced Data Mining, Geospatial Analysis, Network Science, and Recommender Systems.
